The Materiality

Our Craft

We believe that a masterpiece begins in the forest. Our wood is hand-selected from the Dolomites, seasoned for decades under natural conditions to ensure a stability and resonance that modern kilns can never replicate.

From the amber resins of the varnish to the precision of the bridge, every Luwi instrument is a testament to the patient pursuit of acoustic perfection.
